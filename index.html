Trivia Mania!

You will be given 10 multiple choice trivia questions to answer correctly. Each question has one correct and three incorrect answers.

By default, the questions have mixed difficulty and include any categories.

In the settings, you can change the difficulty and genre of the questions.

Your score will increase based on the difficulty of the question.

Easy: + 10 Points

Medium: + 30 Points

Hard: + 50 Points

0 Points
Your Difficulty: Mixed
Your Category: Mixed
